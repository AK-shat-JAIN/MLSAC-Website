Our Faculty Mentor
Dr. Deepika Kumar
Dr. Deepika Kumar, Assistant Professor in Department of Computer Science and Engineering at BVCOE, New Delhi, India. A proactive researcher in the field of Data Science & Machine Learning. She has completed her PhD entitled “Classification of Breast Cancer Histopathology images using Ensemble approach” focused in the field of Computer Science. She completed her M.Tech from NSIT, Delhi and B.Tech from Maharishi Dayanand University, Rohtak. She has a total of 13+ years of Academic/Research Experience and has more than 20 Publications in various National, International Conferences cum International Journals (Scopus/ISI/ SCIE) of high repute.
